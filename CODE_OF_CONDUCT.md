# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional in communication.
- Assume good intent, and give constructive feedback.
- Focus discussions on technical issues and project goals.
- Accept accountability and correct mistakes when they happen.
- Respect differing viewpoints and lived experiences.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or repeated antagonistic behavior.
- Publishing private information without explicit consent.
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces.
- Any conduct that is illegal or deliberately harmful.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, chats, and any other official communication channels for this repository.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to:

- voltsparx@gmail.com

Please include relevant context (links, screenshots, and timestamps if possible). Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as possible.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate for violations, including warning, temporary suspension, removal of content, or permanent ban from project spaces.

## Attribution

This policy is informed by common open-source community standards and adapted for this project.
